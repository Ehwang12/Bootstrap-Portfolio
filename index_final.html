About Me

Esther Hwang is a front-end web developer and illustrator based in snowy Minnesota. She strives to create compelling and engaging apps that enhance her clients' presence and business.

When she's not working on projects you can find Esther exploring second hand bookshops, sketching in a coffeeshop, or working on her fear of falling while rock climbing.
